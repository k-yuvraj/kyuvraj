01_PROJECT_OBJECTIVE

Designed and prototyped a low-cost, efficient grass-cutting machine focusing on ergonomics and sustainability. The primary goal was to integrate modular mechanical components with automated control for precision landscaping.

02_MECHANICAL_ENGINEERING

  • Blade Architecture: Engineered a copper wire blade system for precision cutting efficiency.
  • Structural Integrity: Conducted stress analysis on detailed CAD models to ensure safety and durability.
  • Material Optimization: Utilized lightweight modular components to enhance rover mobility.

03_SYSTEM_INTEGRATION

  • Automation: Integrated battery management and motor drive systems for improved range and performance.
  • Control Systems: Utilized Arduino programming for GPS tracking and hardware-level automation.
